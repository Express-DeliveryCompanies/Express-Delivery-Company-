Our Story Begins with Purpose

Express Delivery was established in 2004 in Brussels, Belgium with a clear goal: to redefine shipping with speed, reliability, and a human touch. From day one, we set out to make global logistics smarter, safer, and more personal connecting families, businesses, and communities with the goods that matter most.

Our mission is simple but powerful: to deliver more than just packages we deliver trust. Whether it's a gift for a loved one, urgent business cargo, or essential supplies, we treat every shipment with care, precision, and dedication.

With an expanding global network, real-time tracking technology, and dedicated customer support, Express Delivery has become a trusted name in cross-border shipping. From warehouses to doorsteps, we make logistics seamless across continents and time zones.

We envision a world where distance doesn't delay dreams. Our commitment is to keep commerce moving, people connected, and promises fulfilled one shipment at a time.
